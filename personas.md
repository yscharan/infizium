# Personas

## 1. Student — Arjun, 14, Class 9

**Background**
Studies at a mid-sized CBSE school in Pune. Lives with both parents. Uses a shared Android phone. Strong in Maths, weak in English writing. Spends evenings on YouTube.

**Goals**
- Understand concepts, not just pass tests.
- Get feedback without feeling judged.
- Know where he stands relative to classmates.

**Pain Points**
- Doubts go unanswered until the next class.
- Doesn't know how to study — just re-reads notes.
- Feels anxious before exams because progress is invisible.

**How Arjun Uses Infizium**
- Submits assignments and gets AI feedback the same evening.
- Asks the AI tutor questions he's too shy to ask in class.
- Tracks his own progress on a personal dashboard.

## 2. Parent — Priya, 40, Mother

**Background**
Works part-time as an accountant. Two children — one in Class 6, one in Class 9. Stays informed via WhatsApp family groups. Does not want to download another school app.

**Goals**
- Know her children are safe, attending, and progressing.
- Be consulted before anything important is decided.
- Not be flooded with irrelevant notifications.

**Pain Points**
- Currently gets information only at PTMs (twice a year).
- Doesn't know who to contact when something feels off.
- Has no visibility into homework or test schedules.

**How Priya Uses Infizium**
- Receives daily attendance updates on WhatsApp.
- Approves or rejects data-sharing permissions from her phone.
- Gets weekly academic summaries as a WhatsApp message.
- Replies "YES" to approve a school trip — no app needed.

## 3. Teacher — Meena, 34, English Teacher

**Background**
Ten years of teaching experience. Classes of 40+ students. Spends two hours a day on non-teaching work — attendance, filling registers, WhatsApp replies to parents. Wants to teach better but lacks time.

**Goals**
- Identify struggling students early.
- Give meaningful feedback without spending hours on correction.
- Communicate with parents without it becoming a second job.

**Pain Points**
- No structured way to track student progress over time.
- Parent messages arrive at all hours with no boundaries.
- Correction and grading are entirely manual.

**How Meena Uses Infizium**
- Creates assignments; AI assists with grading drafts for her review.
- Views a class-level heatmap to spot students falling behind.
- Sends structured parent updates through the platform (not personal WhatsApp).
- Flags students for counselor review with one click.

## 4. School Administrator — Rajesh, 52, Principal

**Background**
Runs a 1,200-student school. Reports to a trust board. Deals with fee collection, compliance, staff management, and parent escalations. Comfortable with spreadsheets but not technical.

**Goals**
- Run the school efficiently without being in every decision.
- Build trust with parents as a transparent institution.
- Demonstrate school performance to the trust board.

**Pain Points**
- No single view of school-wide attendance, fees, or performance.
- Parent complaints arrive without context or history.
- Staff productivity is hard to measure or improve.

**How Rajesh Uses Infizium**
- Views a school health dashboard (attendance, fee collection, academic trends).
- Sees escalations with full context — teacher notes, parent history.
- Generates board-ready reports in one click.
- Configures school-wide communication policies and permissions.
